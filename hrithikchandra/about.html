Meet Our Owner

Hello, my name is Sanjay Parmar and I am the inventor of CHR CINEMAS, a website inspired by my own personal ticket purchase experience. Spending time in long cinema ticket lines was bad, but times when tickets got sold out were the worst. Often, I would return tired with a bad mood, and I knew it was time for a change! So I thought, why is there a need to stand in those long lines and wait for your turn in this modern era of technology? And hence, we are here today. Book tickets for the first show of your favorite movies sitting on your couch without any worry, just in a couple of minutes.
